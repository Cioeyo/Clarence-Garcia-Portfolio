My Journey

I was born and raised in Cavite, where I spent the first 17 years of my life. Growing up in Bacoor, Cavite, I developed a strong connection to my community and family. My parents, Ruben and Nona Garcia, have always been my biggest supporters, encouraging me to pursue my interests and education.

I have two wonderful siblings who have been with me through every step of my journey. My sister Zyra and brother Archie have always been there to support and inspire me.

When I started my Senior High School years, our family moved to Kawit, Cavite. This transition marked an important phase in my life as I began to explore my interests in technology more seriously. It was during this time that I discovered my passion for programming and web development.

After completing Senior High School, I made the decision to move to Estancia, Iloilo to pursue my college education. We have a family house in Tacbuyan, which made this transition smoother. Moving to Iloilo was a significant step in my personal and academic growth, allowing me to become more independent while staying connected to my family roots.

My journey in technology continues to evolve as I study Information Technology at Northern Iloilo State University. Each day brings new challenges and opportunities to learn and grow, and I'm excited to see where this path will lead me.
